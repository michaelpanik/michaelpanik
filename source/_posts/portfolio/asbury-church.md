description: A redesign of [the website for Asbury Church](https://weareasbury.com) in Madison, Alabama. I developed their previous website four years prior. Asbury is a two-campus church with a lot of missions, events, sermons, and other information, so the primary challenge was updating and modernizing, while working to better organize their content.
myRole: I helped design this project alongside an employee. I developed the site, including custom solutions for hero videos throughout the site, over a period of two weeks. Other custom solutions include a sermon archive filtering system and AJAX-based events modal system.
