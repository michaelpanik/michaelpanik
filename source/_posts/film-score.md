description: An app aimed to guide users in rating films objectively, based on a series of focused questions. The end result is a composite score based on a mathematic formula. The project is currently in a completed proof-of-concept phase (app link and login available upon request), working towards a full beta with final UI and feature set at the start of 2019.
myRole: I serve as one half (and the only developer) of Film Score. This is a passion project stemming out of a dream my friend and I have had for years. I have developed the entire pre-beta from scratch, and am continuing to design and develop the final application. The screens below are from the proof of concept, and the final design system (made in Adobe XD).
